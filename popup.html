Projected Balance

for Monarch Money · unofficial

Adds a forward-looking projected balance graph, an outstanding-items register, and a confidence-gated reconciler to your Monarch account pages.

Open any account in Monarch and the projection appears beneath the Current balance graph. Use + Add under Pending Transactions to log a check or transfer that hasn't posted yet.

Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Monarch Money. Your register is stored in your browser (synced or local, your choice) and is never sent to the author or any third party.
